🔐 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Binai on Lightchain

Your identity

Your wallet address is your Binai account ID. An optional PIN locks the app on your device only — stored locally, keyed to your wallet address, not on our server.

What we store

Display name, language, preferences, memories, notes, reminders, chat history, subscription/usage records. Stored on Railway cloud infrastructure.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not read your mic until you tap the mic button. Voice is converted to text by your browser/device where supported.

Your controls

Export or delete all your data anytime in Settings → Your data.
